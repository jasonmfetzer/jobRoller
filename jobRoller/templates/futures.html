Our aim with this application is to make it easier for IIT students to navigate course selection and create cohesion in the job application process. Students should be able to take courses of interest while also being able to fulfill the IIT course requirements for the Bachelor of Science.

Goals of Scalability

  1. Python Package Index (Reuse)
  2. Scalable to MS Programs and Related Depts. (i.e. SAT)
  3. Full Text Search Course of Interest
